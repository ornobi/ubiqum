About NYSL

Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills team cooperation and good sportmaship.

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop storng, well-rounded, and mindful athletes trough the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northern neigborhoods an environment in wich to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in Chicago northside neigborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
